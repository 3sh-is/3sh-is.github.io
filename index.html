Heimsmeistari í þríþraut ráðinn þjálfari hjá 3SH 4 nóvember, 2015

3SH kynnir með stolti ráðningu Fred Van Lierde heimsmeistara í Ironman 2013 sem yfirþjálfara fyrir árið 2016 frá og með janúar næstkomandi.

Fred er reyndur þríþrautarþjálfarari og ráðning hans markar tímamót í sögu 3SH. Fred hefur sannað sig sem einn besti þríþrautarmaður heims í heilum og hálfum járnmanni. Hann mun njóta aðstoðar Vincent Van Lierde bróður síns sem er hokinn af keppnisreynslu í ýmsum vegalengdum þríþrautar.

3SH býður þá velkomna til starfa.

3SH is proud to announce that for the 2016 the team has hired Fred Van Lierde, professional triathlete, as head coach through the upcoming season starting this January.

Fred has great experience in coaching triathletes and his work will be a milestone in 3SH´s history. Fred has proven himself as one of the best triathletes in the world in the Ironman distance as well as half Ironman. Fred will be assisted by his brother Vincent Van Lierde who has competed in various distances in triathlon.

3SH welcomes them on board.
